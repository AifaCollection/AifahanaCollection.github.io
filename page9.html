It's time for us to rock the bawal and give it a fresh new look!
There are tons of colours to choose from because AINAA will be available in shades. With a whole spectrum of hues, you won't encounter any problems finding the perfect scarf to match every outfit in your wardrobe. We've kept the AINAA simple and minimal, only a a discreet metal tag adorn one corner of the scarf, this makes it an awesome choice for any ensemble.
AINAA will appeal to everyone, it is an evergreen design, no matter who you are, with this collection, you'll be an absolute stunner. Young fashionistas, mature career women or even busy mothers, make it a part of your style arsenal, you can never go wrong with AINAA by A.H.

Faiqa is a square scarf that is very basic with cotton wool material.
The name fits the bill perfectly because Faiqais a clean and uncomplicated design, with only one adornment gracing the plain hijab, consisting of a metal circle plate placed on one corner of the scarf to give it that signaturer Aifahana Hijab touch.
Although the square is large enough to be folded twice to give you an extra layer, cotton has low opacity so it's best to wear the Faiqa with an inner to hide your silhouette and keep you fully covered.
Faiqa has a wonderfully vintage feel to it with an airy and fairy-tale like quality that makes it the quintessential hijab traditional garb. It's also ultra-fine and lightweight so you can remain immaculate even during those blistering hot days.
